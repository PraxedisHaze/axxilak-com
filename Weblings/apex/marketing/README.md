## NOTES FOR TIMOTHY

### Why This Approach

The core message you provided ("Free, custom, professional website with functional contact me...") is strong. I expanded it into multiple variations because:

1. **Different contexts need different length/tone**
   - Hero section: 1-2 sentences (visual impact)
   - FAQ: Full explanation (addressing concerns)
   - Social media: Short and punchy (attention span)

2. **Multiple angles convert different people**
   - "Own it forever" resonates with people burned by subscriptions
   - "No coding" resonates with non-technical founders
   - "Free" resonates with budget-conscious
   - "Professional" resonates with business image concerns

3. **Love First quality**
   - No manipulative marketing language
   - No false urgency ("Limited time!")
   - No hype ("Revolutionary!" "AI-powered!")
   - Just honest benefits
